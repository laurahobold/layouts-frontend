about us
who we are

The Grand Narukami Shrine is a religious organization that worships the Electro Archon and leads the Narukami people's faith. The shrine is located on Mt. Yougou, Inazuma. The use of military force is not permitted around the areas of the Grand Narukami Shrine as it dishonors the Shogun herself, thus the shrine is not under the jurisdiction of the Shogunate but still under the authority of the Electro Archon whom the shrine worships.
